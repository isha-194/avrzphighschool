About AVR ZP High School

AVR ZP High School, located in Edlapalli, Tsundur Mandal, Guntur District, Andhra Pradesh, offers grades 6 to 10 in both English and Telugu mediums. It is a co-educational institution with a large campus and extensive grounds. The school conducts yearly sports events to promote physical fitness and teamwork. The academic schedule starts at 9 AM and concludes at 5 PM, with every class from grades 6 to 10 having four hours dedicated to sports each week. Apart from academics, the school encourages extracurricular activities such as drawing, dancing, singing, essay writing, sports, and various cultural activities, ensuring the holistic development of its students and preparing them for both academic and personal success.
