Admissions

Admissions for grades 6 to 10 at AVR ZP High School take place from May to the first week of June. We are pleased to inform you that there are no admission fees or school fees. To enroll, parents need to fill out the admission form available at the school office, along with submitting the previous academic records and identification documents. Admission is granted based on eligibility and availability of seats. We encourage early applications to secure a place for your child.
